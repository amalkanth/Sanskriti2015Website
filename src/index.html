About MACE

Kothamangalam, a place better known as "the gateway to western Ghats" is not only a land known for fables and churches. This place owns one of the best engineering colleges in the state,the Mar Athanasius College of Engineering (MACE). The college for the past 52 years has constantly produced renowned personalities in the field of engineering. The first ever Christian management college in Asia, and the first college to have enrolled in the Mahatma Gandhi University, with over six branches of engineering and five post graduate courses, 3000 students presently being trained and over 12,000 notable alumni, MACE proudly holds the record and continues to make new ones. MACE also hosts a lush green ambience beautified by the symphonies of Mozart and Beethoven from the garden speakers that infuses a calm, serene and refresh, ing environment. The college promotes a wide variety of activities, be it educational or cultural and the different clubs play an important role in bringing out the best in the students. Every bit of information that a student could possibly wish for, is provided by the department libraries, state of the art workshops, I. T. centre and the central library that houses a mammoth collection of journals and papers thus it could be said that life at MACE is truly a dream college life for every student.
